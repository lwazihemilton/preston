Legal Resources

Access key South African legal documents and government portals for your legal needs.

  • Consumer Protection Act 68 of 2008 - Governs consumer rights and fair business practices.
  • Companies Act 71 of 2008 - Regulates company formation and governance.
  • Divorce Act 70 of 1979 - Outlines divorce and related family law matters.
  • Deeds Registries Act 47 of 1937 - Governs property registration.
  • Wills Act 7 of 1953 - Regulates the drafting of wills.
  • Criminal Procedure Act 51 of 1977 - Guides criminal proceedings.
  • Legal Practice Act 28 of 2014 - Regulates the legal profession.
  • Companies and Intellectual Property Commission (CIPC) - For company and IP registration.
  • SA Government Documents - Comprehensive repository of legislation and forms.
  • Legal Practice Council - Verify law firms and attorneys.
