Our South African Legal Services

Family Law

Guidance on divorce, maintenance, and child custody under the Divorce Act 70 of 1979.

Criminal Law

Defence in criminal cases, including bail applications, per the Criminal Procedure Act 51 of 1977.

Commercial Law

Support for business contracts and compliance under the Companies Act 71 of 2008.

Property Law

Assistance with conveyancing and disputes under the Deeds Registries Act 47 of 1937.

Delict (Tort)

Claims for damages, including personal injury, under South African common law.

Estate Planning

Drafting wills and trusts under the Wills Act 7 of 1953 and Trust Property Control Act 57 of 1988.
